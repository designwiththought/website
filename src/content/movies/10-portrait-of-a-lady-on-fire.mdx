Annual rewatch. The page-28 scene, always.

I have a small list of films that I rewatch on a schedule rather than on a whim, because they reward the calendar. This is one of them. Each year the page-28 scene reads differently — slightly more about looking, slightly less about being looked at — and that is the gift of a film that has decided to be patient.
