A ghost story that is really a story about carrying. Held up better a week later than it did the night of.

I left the cinema unsure of the ending and I think that was the point — the kind of film that asks you to sleep on it. A week on, I had a clearer sense of what it had done to me, which is rare for a film that uses this much sentiment this directly.
