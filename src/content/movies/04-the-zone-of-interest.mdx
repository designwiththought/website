Sound design as moral architecture. The hardest film I have watched in a decade. I will not rewatch it soon.

The sound mix is doing the thing the picture refuses to do, which is to insist that what is offscreen is the entire point. I have not stopped thinking about it; I am also not in a hurry to sit through it again. Both of those reactions are, I think, what the film is asking for.
