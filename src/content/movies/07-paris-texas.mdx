Revisited after Perfect Days. The long booth scene is still the best scene of dialogue in cinema, for my money.

Watched back-to-back with Perfect Days as a kind of personal Wenders bookend. The two films are forty years apart and yet they are obviously the same person looking at the same problem, which is what it means for a body of work to add up to one long argument.
