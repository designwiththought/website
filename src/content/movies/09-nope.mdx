Rewatched on 35mm at the Roxie. The cinema is a character; that is the whole thesis.

I had filed this one away as the lesser of the three Peeles after the first watch and I was wrong. On the big screen, with the projector noise behind me, the film's argument about looking and being looked at lands much harder. A film about cinema deserves to be seen in one.
