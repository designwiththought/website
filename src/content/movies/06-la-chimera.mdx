Rohrwacher keeps making films I want to live inside. A quiet one, but the kind where the light is doing all the work.

The 16mm grain is part of the argument, and so is the way the actors carry themselves as though the camera is the third or fourth most important presence in the room. I admire films that trust their texture to do more than the script does, and this is a textbook on that trust.
