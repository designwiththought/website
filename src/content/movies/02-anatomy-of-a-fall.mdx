The best film about language I have seen in years. Every witness is also translating.

Two languages on screen and a third one — the language the marriage spoke at home — that the film keeps almost showing you and then withholding. The court scenes work because every line of dialogue is also a small act of editing, which is what I think we all do to our marriages from the inside.
