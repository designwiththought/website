Recorded live to tape in a cabin, and you can hear the walls. The kind of album that does not help you feel better, just feel it on purpose.

I went into this one on a hard week and came out the other side with no particular wisdom, only a clearer sense of what the week had cost. That is what the record is for. The tape hiss is not a flaw; it is the room agreeing to be in the song.
