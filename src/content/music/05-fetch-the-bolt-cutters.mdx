The album that taught me percussion is a thought, not an accompaniment. Still unbeaten as a study in what a voice can carry.

I had thought I knew how to listen to a record before this one, and then it taught me that what I had been doing was hearing. The drums are arguing; the rooms are arguing; even the dog is arguing. The whole thing is the sound of someone deciding that polish is not the same as care.
