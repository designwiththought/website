The first album I paid for with my own money, in the pay-what-you-want days. Still finds new ways to be the right record.

I paid eight pounds and felt like I was overspending, which says more about being seventeen than about the music. The album has done so much work since then that the price seems comical now. Records earn their keep; this one paid me back many times over.
