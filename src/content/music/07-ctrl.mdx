Went in expecting a hit album, left with a slow burn. "20 Something" is still the closer.

The radio singles are the front door, but the rooms past the front door are what kept me here. The closer in particular keeps moving as I get older — I heard a different song at 24 than I heard at 30, and I expect to hear another one at 40.
