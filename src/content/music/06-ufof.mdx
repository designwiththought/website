The record I reach for when I need the world to slow down by about 20%. Adrianne and Buck, again.

There is a particular tempo that this band trusts, and it is just slightly behind where you think the song should be. The first listen this can read as hesitation; the tenth listen it reads as deliberation. By the hundredth listen you have started timing your own week against it.
