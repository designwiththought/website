A lesson in refusing genre. I play "Heavy Heart" the night before hard meetings.

The record sounds like someone who has decided that the question "what kind of artist are you" is the wrong question, and answered it by writing a track that earns the right to follow whichever idea it found first. I keep coming back to the courage of that decision more than to any single song.
