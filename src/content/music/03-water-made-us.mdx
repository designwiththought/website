Wrote most of the first year at Superhuman to this. Soft enough to not intrude, dense enough to reward attention when you give it.

Some albums are companions while you do something else, and some albums are the something else. This one drifts between the two roles depending on the day, which is the trick. The lyrics are doing real work, and the production trusts you to find the work without underlining it.
