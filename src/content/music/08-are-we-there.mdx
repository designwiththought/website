Soundtracked a move from London to the Bay. "Your Love is Killing Me" did most of the lifting.

Some albums attach themselves to a season, and some attach themselves to a relocation. I cannot listen to the long centre track without a specific stretch of motorway turning up in my head, which is the test I think of when I argue that records and places are the same memory in two formats.
