I was 14, and my older cousin handed me a burned CD. Changed what I thought a record could feel like on headphones.

There is a particular kind of confidence that this album taught me to listen for, which I did not have a name for at the time. The samples do most of the introduction, but the writing is the thing I came back to once I knew enough to come back to writing. The whole record is paced like a conversation that is sure of itself.
