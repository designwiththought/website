A PM described our new flow as "frictionless" in the brief. I have started replacing the word with what it usually means: "we haven't timed it yet."

Most flows have friction. The question is whose, and where. A flow that is frictionless for a designer at a whiteboard is often quite abrasive for a stranger at 11pm on a flaky connection.
