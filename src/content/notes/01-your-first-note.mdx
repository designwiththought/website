A note is a short fragment — usually one paragraph, sometimes two. The first paragraph becomes the lede on the reader page; subsequent paragraphs become the afterthought.

Notes are grouped by `month:` on `/notes/` and surface as the four most recent on the homepage's "From the notebook" section. Earlier / Later navigation between notes uses file order.
