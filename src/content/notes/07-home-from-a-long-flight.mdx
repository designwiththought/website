Home from a long flight. In-flight entertainment: remains the worst-designed software most people ever touch. I wrote three pages in the margin of a paperback about it.

Everything about it fights the user — the tap targets, the response times, the focus states that do not exist, the volume control that is three menus deep. If I ever run out of things to care about professionally, I will go work on an airline's seat-back screen and count it as a retirement project.
