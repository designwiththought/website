Second read. The chapter on the hand is doing most of the work.

A first read of Sennett asks you to nod along; the second one asks you to argue. I am a few chapters in and already filling the margins with names of engineers and designers I want to hand pages to. The book treats craft as a moral category as much as a technical one, which is the part that keeps me coming back.
