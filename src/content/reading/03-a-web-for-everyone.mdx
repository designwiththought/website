Rereading for a workshop.

The book is older than a lot of the engineers I work with and it still reads as a generous, opinionated checklist of what good looks like. I am pulling chapters apart for an internal session and finding that the bits that look most dated — the screen-reader transcripts especially — are the bits that teach the most.
