The only management book I trust on migrations.

Most management books treat migrations as an organisational footnote. This one treats them as a practice with a literature, and the practice gets sharper for it. I refer back to the section on "the cost of a useful migration" once a quarter, and I have copied entire diagrams onto whiteboards to argue points in planning rooms.
