I have given away four copies.

The book reads like a colleague who has been doing the work for ten years and is now writing down the bits she wishes someone had told her. It is short, opinionated, and unusually generous with the small operational details — meeting structures, one-on-one rhythms, feedback scripts. I keep buying copies and giving them away.
