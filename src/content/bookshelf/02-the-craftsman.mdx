The book that let me stop apologising for spending time on small things.

Sennett gave me a vocabulary for something I had been doing for years without a name for it. Before this book, the slow part of the work felt like a guilty pleasure; after it, I could call it the work itself. That kind of relabelling is rare in a book, and worth keeping a copy on the shelf for the next time I need it.
