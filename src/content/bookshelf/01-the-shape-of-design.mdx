Still the best short book on what designing is. A yearly reread.

A book that fits in a back pocket and takes a year to digest. I keep three copies in the house at any given time so that when a junior asks me to recommend something I can hand one over without thinking. It has earned its small permanent spot on every shelf I have moved between cities.
