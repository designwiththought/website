Comfort reading, the best kind.

A novel that has decided to be patient with itself, and trusts the reader to be patient too. The single-hotel constraint is the structural joke and also the quiet moral argument: a small life attended to carefully is no smaller than a large life attended to badly. I reread chunks when I am tired.
