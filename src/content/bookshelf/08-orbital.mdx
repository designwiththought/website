A short, quiet novel set on the ISS. Made me want to rewrite every product brief with more attention to the view.

Sixteen orbits, six astronauts, and a writer whose attention is so steady that the small movements of the day become the entire plot. I read it on a long flight, which is the right way to meet a book that has decided that altitude is the only structural device it needs.
