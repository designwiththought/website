Changed what I thought the novel could do in structural terms.

Eight short stories that braid into a single argument is a hard book to make, and most attempts at this trick collapse halfway through the second act. This one earns the structure by trusting the reader to do half the connecting. I have not stopped thinking about the last hundred pages, which is what I want from a novel.
