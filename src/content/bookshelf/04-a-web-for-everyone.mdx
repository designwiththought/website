I reread the chapter on plain language every time I write a PRD.

Older than most accessibility books still in print and somehow still the most useful. The personas chapter has aged the least gracefully, but the framework has aged the best. I have a personal habit of opening it before any new accessibility programme and rereading the same eight pages, which is the highest praise I can give a manual.
