Older than I am; still right.

The book that explains why so much else of the genre exists. Grove was writing in a manufacturing context and yet most of the book transposes cleanly to software work, which is a tribute to how carefully he chose his abstractions. The chapter on one-on-ones is still the only one I would assign as required reading.
