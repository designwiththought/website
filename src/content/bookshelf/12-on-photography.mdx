Read it at 22. Reread it at 32. Rereading it at 42.

A book that meets you at whatever age you are. At 22 it read as a permission slip; at 32 it read as a warning; at 42 it reads as a quiet conversation between the other two readings. I plan to keep this on a roughly decade rhythm and see what it does to me at 52.
