The best craft podcast of the last ten years. Every episode is a masterclass in one thing at a time.

The format is a single song, the artists who made it, and a host who knows when to keep his mouth shut. That last bit is the rare thing. I have learned more about how to interview from this show than from any book on the subject, which is to say: stay out of the way, and the work will speak for itself.
