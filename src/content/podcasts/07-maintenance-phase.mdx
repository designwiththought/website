When I need a takedown of a wellness fad, done well.

The two-host chemistry is the format here, and the research is the thing under the format. They commit to actually reading the paper, which is a low bar that most of their genre fails to clear. I queue them up when a relative has fallen for a new diet, and I learn something every time even when I thought I came in informed.
