The craft interviews are the good ones. I skip the news segments.

The format has shifted across the years and not every change has been a win, but the craft interview is still the thing they do best of any podcast in the field. Two designers, one piece of work, and a willingness to spend forty minutes on a single decision — that is the whole pitch and it still earns the time.
