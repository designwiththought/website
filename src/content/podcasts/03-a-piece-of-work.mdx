Short episodes on contemporary art. Perfect dishwashing length.

A short-run series with a particular kind of generosity to it — the host is curious without being precious, and the format respects the listener's time. I have been going back through it slowly while doing chores, and the episodes get better when you do not pay total attention, which is a counterintuitive compliment.
