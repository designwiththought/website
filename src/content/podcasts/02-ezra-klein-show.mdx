For the long-form interviews. The best interviewer working in American media right now.

The thing he does that nobody else is doing is preparing as if the guest is the smartest person in the room and then arguing as if he might be. That balance is unusual and it produces the only American interview show I trust to teach me something about a topic I thought I already knew.
