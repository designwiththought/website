The body of an essay can use markdown — paragraphs, headings, lists, blockquotes, inline `code`, and links. It can also use the four MDX components defined in `src/components/`: `<Dropcap>`, `<Pullquote>`, `<PullquoteNoCite>`, and `<MarginaliaPin>`.

## Section two

You can drop a `<MarginaliaPin>` next to a paragraph for a handwritten margin note that appears in the right gutter on wide viewports and hides below 1200px.

To add another essay, drop a new `.mdx` file in `src/content/articles/` with `kind: Essay`. It'll appear at `/essays/<slug>/` automatically. Set `kind: Study` to land it at `/studies/<slug>/` instead.
