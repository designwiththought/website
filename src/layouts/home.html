I'm Brian Hinton, accessibility lead at Superhuman and formerly at Grammarly. I spend most of my time thinking about what happens when you treat design as a form of care: for the people using the software, for the engineers building it, and for the systems that hold it all together.

For years my online presence was rented space. Social profiles and digital CVs on platforms I didn't own. This site is me building a home on the internet instead. The foundation is my own, the creaky floorboards are ones I coded, and the front door is always open. It's a place to slow down and share ideas that invite reflection rather than consumption, on design systems, accessibility, leadership, and whatever I'm paying attention to now.
