Morning light on Mount Hood

March 10, 2026

Caught this view at sunrise from Jonsrud Viewpoint, just outside Sandy, Oregon.

I was driving out to a trailhead early Saturday morning when the light caught my eye. Mount Hood was lit up in this impossible pink-gold, the kind of color you get for maybe ten minutes if you're lucky.

Jonsrud Viewpoint is one of those spots every Oregonian knows but somehow never feels crowded. A parking lot, a bench, and one of the best views of Hood you can get without climbing anything.

Shot on my phone. No edits. Sometimes the best camera really is the one you have with you.1
